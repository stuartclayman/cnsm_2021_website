EI 2021

1st International Workshop on Edge Intelligence

The focus of EI2021 is to provide an overview of the works carried out so far in the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques applied at the Edge of 5G&B and the 6G future networks.

Paper Submissions:
Authors are invited to submit original unpublished papers not under review elsewhere. Submissions will be subjected to a peer-review process. Regular papers should be submitted in IEEE 2-column format, with paper length up to 7 pages including references.
