CNSM 2021 Registration

We have re-structured the registration fees as CNSM 2021 will be hybrid – on-site and remote – to accommodate all travel and health constraints.

Registration opens on 25 August 2021. Author registration deadline is 11 September 2021 for the main track, and 21 September 2021 for the Workshops. Other registrations open on 25 August 2021, with 1 Oct 2021 as the deadline for early registration.

Author Registration and Attendance Policy

  • Every paper accepted at CNSM 2021, and the workshops must have an associated registration at the Author rate.
  • For each paper – this applies for all Full and Short (poster) papers of CNSM 2021, mini conference and workshops included – at least one co-author must be registered as author (Author rate) to secure the paper’s publication.
  • All presenters must be registered either at Author rate, or as participant if the paper is already covered with another Author registration.
  • The registration of a single co-author as author (Author rate) will secure the publication of up to two papers from the same co-author.
  • All papers have to be presented by the paper co-authors. CNSM 2021 reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g., removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper presentation condition is not met.
  • All the Registration Fees (rates) given in the tables below are in US Dollars.
