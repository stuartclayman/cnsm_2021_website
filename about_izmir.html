About Izmir

Izmir is the third largest city in Turkey and the second biggest port after Istanbul. Izmir's origins began at the ancient city of Smyrna. Today it is a modern, developed, and busy commercial center surrounded by mountains and a huge bay.

Izmir is the third largest city in Turkey and the second biggest port after Istanbul. Izmir's origins began at the ancient city of Smyrna. Today it is a modern, developed, and busy commercial center surrounded by mountains and a huge bay.

Izmir has treasures like the ancient city of Ephesus, which is on the UNESCO World Heritage List, and the remnants of the Temple of Artemis, which is one of the Seven Wonders of the world.
